## The Real Reason Behind "Why My Website Isn't Ranking"

Google calls this set of signals **Core Web Vitals** — three specific metrics that measure loading speed, responsiveness, and visual stability. They sound technical, but the impact is very simple:

- A page that loads in 5+ seconds loses visitors before your offer ever appears.
- A layout that jumps around while loading makes visitors tap the wrong button — or leave.
- A page that lags when someone taps "Get a Quote" quietly costs you the lead.

None of this shows up as a red flag in your content editor. It shows up as high bounce rates, lower rankings, and a lead form that gets far fewer submissions than your traffic numbers suggest it should.

## Why This Matters More If You Want to Generate Leads Without Ads

Paid ads can mask a slow website — you're paying to force traffic through a leaky funnel. But if your goal is to **generate leads without ads**, or reduce your dependency on them, your website's technical experience has to carry the full weight of conversion. There's no ad budget compensating for a page that takes 6 seconds to show your headline.

This is precisely why **SEO for startups** has to include technical page experience, not just keywords and blog posts. A startup with lean marketing budgets gets the most leverage from organic, compounding traffic — but only if the website itself doesn't quietly sabotage it.
