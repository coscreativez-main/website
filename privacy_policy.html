PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how our digital and content marketing company COS Creatives ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, protects, utilizes, and processes the information you provide when interacting with our website and utilizing our marketing solutions.

1. Information We Collect and Purpose of Use

When you subscribe to our newsletters, fill out quote request forms, download resources, or submit inquiries on our website, we collect user-submitted details which may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title.

Marketing Purposes:

The core purpose of collecting this information is to enhance your experience and deliver tailored marketing services. Specifically, we use your details to:

  • Send targeted marketing emails, newsletters, and promotional campaign materials.
  • Deliver customized content marketing updates, case studies, and industry insights.
  • Reach out regarding standard business development, lead nurturing, and service offerings.
  • Optimize our advertising efforts and personalize your journey on our website.

Our Commitment:

We will never misuse, sell, rent, or abuse the personal data collected from our users. Your information is accessed solely by authorized organizational personnel for legitimate marketing and business operations.

2. Third-Party Data Sharing and Tools

We respect your privacy. We explicitly guarantee that your personal data will not be shared with, sold to, or distributed to any unauthorized third parties for their independent commercial use.

However, to deliver seamless services and manage data effectively, we utilize industry-standard third-party software and infrastructure providers. Your data is processed through, but not stored by outside parties independent of, the following tools:

  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Systems: To safely organize, store, track, and maintain consumer communications and professional relationships.
  • Google Tag Manager (GTM) & Analytics: To evaluate website performance, track user actions, and accurately configure analytical and optimization updates.
  • Email Marketing & Automation Software: To efficiently dispatch digital newsletters, content assets, and targeted promotional updates.

These third-party platforms function strictly as data processors acting under our instructions. They are bound by stringent confidentiality agreements and standard data protection requirements to ensure your details remain protected.

3. Cookies and Automated Tracking Technologies (New Essential Element)

In addition to the information you manually submit, our website utilizes "cookies" and similar tracking mechanisms via Google Tag Manager (GTM) to automatically gather certain technical data. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and your behavior on our web pages.

We use this information to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movements around the platform, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level, though disabling them may limit your use of certain features or functions on our website.

4. Data Retention Policy (New Essential Element)

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the marketing purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. If you are a client or active subscriber, we retain your data to maintain our ongoing business relationship. If you opt-out or unsubscribe from our marketing materials, we will immediately remove you from our active marketing flows, though your email may be kept on a suppression list in our CRM to ensure we honor your opt-out request and do not contact you again.

5. International Data Transfers (New Essential Element)

Because we use global third-party tools (such as US-based CRMs and analytics platforms), the information we collect may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in a country outside of your home jurisdiction. By submitting your details through our website, you consent to this transfer, storage, and processing. We ensure all external processors maintain adequate security measures that match or exceed local data protection standards.

6. Children's Privacy Protection (New Essential Element)

Our digital and content marketing services are strictly directed to business professionals and individuals over the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from anyone under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that information from our databases as swiftly as possible.

7. Cybersecurity, Theft, and Inherent Risks

We employ robust technological and organizational security safeguards designed to insulate your data from unauthorized modifications, damage, or exposures. Despite our proactive protocols, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic infrastructure is completely foolproof.

Disclaimer of Inherent Electronic Risk: While we utilize robust infrastructure to prevent vulnerabilities, there is always an inherent risk of unauthorized data theft, sophisticated malicious attacks, zero-day security gaps, and general cyber threats. By submitting your details, you acknowledge that you provide your personal data at your own calculated discretion.

8. Your Rights and Preferences

You maintain ultimate control over how we contact you for marketing objectives. Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR or CCPA frameworks), you possess the following rights:

  • Opt-Out / Unsubscribe: You may cancel or halt marketing messages at any moment by clicking the "Unsubscribe" hyperlink embedded at the footer of our emails.
  • Data Access and Upkeep: You may request to review, rectify, or request the erasure of your personal data stored within our active CRM systems.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a digital, machine-readable format.

9. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy as our business requirements and international data regulations evolve. Any adjustments made will be signaled directly by modifying the effective date visible at the top of this document.

Contact Information: If you have inquiries, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is processed by our marketing tools, please reach out via our website's contact form.
